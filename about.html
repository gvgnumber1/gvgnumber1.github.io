so, what is gvg?

The hybrid process

What makes for an efficient process?

Every client wants a unique process to them, but setting one up for efficient multi-tasking is imperative for a team's collaboration. Patrón's seasonal programs are massive and required integrating the production, design, retouching and copyediting components of a typical operation. I crafted this process, built for addressing these challenges of integrated production and design, to better collaborate across an entire team.

Blending Production and Design Together

  • Evaluate project scope, providing any unit estimates and route them for approvals.
  • Advise on planning, resources and timing while setting expectations for needs to achieve these goals.
  • Intake and review creative assets and vendor materials for missing details.
  • Prioritize elements around all appropriate factors and delegate elements by priority and skill level.
  • Collaborate with Creative on the program's KV, finalizing every detail and their impact on the rest of the scope.
  • Retouch and composite the KV until undeniably perfected, then adapt out across all elements.
  • Multi-task first production proofs, routing and quick turn revisions to keep operations smooth.
  • Communicate needs that reduce hurdles for upcoming elements and any timeline impacts they cause.
  • Process final approvals as they come through for organization and release.
  • Submit final units reporting to finance for applicable elements.
  • Evaluate the measured statistics for successes and areas of improvement. Applying findings to the next planning phase.
  • And of course, being at various points in this process for multiple jobs at the same time.
