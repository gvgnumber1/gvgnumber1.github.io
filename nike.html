In November of 2020, I put together the final touches of the Nike Zine for South Side: Chicago. As always, great work takes many talented people coming together to contribute all their talents. My role on this project was to take the art direction from Dylan Bannecke and produce a perfect and print-ready file for production. This included composition cleanup, image retouching, revisions and file release.

The Zine celebrates the opening of a community store in the South Side of Chicago for Nike, by focusing on local artists making an impact in that community. It celebrates the culture, achievement and legacy of this historic area of Chicago.
