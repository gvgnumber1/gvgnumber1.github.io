Charleston Battery

Stadium Launch
Freelance Production Specialist for creative studio Common Goal
In Spring 2020, I helped the creative studio Common Goal launch 24 outdoor signage elements to brand Patriots Point soccer stadium for the Charleston Battery. From the parking lot to the stadium seats, fans and players see the city's pride in their soccer team. These elements address 7 major zones of Patriots’ Point: Main Entrance, Player Entrance, East Bleacher, West Bleacher, Supporter Bleacher, Fencing and the Back of House. Communication was key, as I stepped up to intake vendor requests and provide solutions based off my own experience producing stadium signage for NFL, NBA, MLB and NHL stadium advertising.
Patriots' Point looking West towards the Cooper River, just before it meets the Atlantic Ocean. This is about 11 miles from where I spent the first few years of my life.
